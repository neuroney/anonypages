Deploy to Cloudflare Pages

Step 1: Sign up for Cloudflare

If you don't have a Cloudflare account, go to cloudflare.com and create one. It's free!

Step 2: Access Cloudflare Pages

After logging in, click on 'Pages' in the left sidebar of your Cloudflare dashboard.

Step 3: Create a New Project

Click the 'Create a project' button and select 'Direct Upload' as the deployment method.

Step 4: Upload Your Files

Step 4.1: Enter Project Name

Enter your project name. Your repository will be accessible at [project-name].pages.dev

Step 4.2: Click to Upload

Click the upload area to select your ZIP file

Step 4.3: Upload in Progress

Wait while your files are being uploaded

Step 4.4: Upload Complete

Your files have been successfully uploaded

Step 5: Share Your Site

Once deployment is complete, Cloudflare will provide you with a URL. You can share this URL with your paper reviewers. Note: It may take about 10 minutes for your site to be fully accessible after deployment.
